---
title: "Recent Releases"
description: "Endpoint for recent releasees of anime episodes"
---

Get a list of recently added episodes

## Base URL

```
https://dramx.vercel.app/api/anime/recent
```
---
## Params

None

---

## Example

<code>https://dramx.vercel.app/api/anime/recent</code>
